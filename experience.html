My Experience

I have been a fan of F1 since I was a child. I grew up in Bahrain and would often go watch the F1 with my family.

Watching it live is such a great experience, the cars are unbelievably loud and the sheer speed they are driving is ridiculous.

Not only did I watch the F1 live but for a school trip we went to the F1 track and the busses we were on took us for a ride around the track.

If you think that was cool, read this....There is a building next to the track and on the very top floor is the kings chair for when he is watching live.

All of us kids that went on the trip got a turn to sit in the kings chair.
